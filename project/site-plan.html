Accra Home Renters

Site Name: accra-homerenters.com

This site name name tells prospective users that the website will provide information on home rentals without even having to read the site description.

Site Purpose:

The site will aim to reduce the hassles of looking for a home to rent in Accra. It will host a catalogue of homes available for rental in Accra and provide relevant contact details of the property owners and a form to book for viewing.

Scenarios:

  • What are the rent regulations in Accra?
  • What is the average rent in Accra?

Color Schema

  • Light cyan - Headers and footers
  • Alice blue - body background

Typography:

Roboto font will be used for the entire page

Sans-Serif will be used for browsers that do not support Roboto
